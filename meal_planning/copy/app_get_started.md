## Getting Started with Palate

### 1. Build Your Palette
Browse the dishes in **Your Palette** on the left. Use the search and filter to find dishes you love.

### 2. Create Your Canvas
Click the arrow to add dishes to **Your Canvas** on the right. Aim for variety across cuisines and categories.

### 3. See Your Colors
Hit **See Your Colors** to analyze your meal plan's diversity. You'll see:
- Category coverage across weeks
- Cuisine variety
- East/West balance

### Tips
- **Mix it up**: Try dishes from different cuisines each week
- **Cover your bases**: Include greens, proteins, and grains
- **Have fun**: There's no wrong way to eat well
